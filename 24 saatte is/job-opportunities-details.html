İş Tanımı

We are looking for frontend and backend developers who will help us realize our vision to go global. Come join us if you have hunger for learning and a passion for making an impact! Be part of something bigger than yourself. Looking forward to meeting you.

Aranan Nitelikler

5+ years experience as a developer (for Seniors)

3+ years experience with JavaScript and React / React Native (for Seniors)

Excellent knowledge of JavaScript with data structures, algorithms and software design principles

Knowledge of ESNext features

Good knowledge of typing and tools such as TypeScript and Flow

Good knowledge of mobile application guidelines and their ecosystem

Experience in testing and its tools such as Jest, Mocha

Feeling comfortable with strict linting rules

Knowledge of functional and object-oriented programming principle

Knowledge of native modules (bridge) is a plus

Knowledge of Firebase products is a plus

Experience in using Git for source control

Bonus point if you have experience with Fastlane

Bonus point if you know or have experience in Agile software development lifecycle (code review, pair programming, continuous delivery, continuous integration, TDD, BDD etc.)
